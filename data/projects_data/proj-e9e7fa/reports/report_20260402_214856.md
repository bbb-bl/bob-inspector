1. Executive Summary
On 02 April 2026, a construction safety inspection was conducted at the Downtown Condo project located at Provenca. The inspection revealed that while some critical safety items have been addressed, several key concerns remain outstanding, posing risks to the health and safety of workers on site. Overall, 5 out of 8 checklist items were completed, with 2 critical items still pending.

2. Critical Findings
- Location: Third floor, open edge; Risk: High risk of fall incident; Recommendation: Install fall protection immediately to prevent falls from the open edge.
- Location: Temporary electrical distribution panel; Risk: Risk of electric shock and short circuit; Recommendation: Ensure all electrical installations are covered and protected with proper conduits.
- Location: Scaffolding; Risk: Potential structural failure; Recommendation: Inspect and certify scaffolding to ensure it is safe for use.

3. Completed Checks
- Personal protective equipment (PPE) available on site
- Emergency exit routes clearly marked and unobstructed
- Fire extinguishers present and in-date
- Waste and debris removed from walkways
- Machinery safety guards in place

4. Outstanding Items
- Critical: Scaffolding inspected and certified
- Critical: Fall protection installed at open edges (floor 3)
- High: Electrical installations covered and protected

5. Recommendations
Based on the inspection findings, the following recommendations are made:
- Immediate installation of fall protection at the open edge on the third floor to mitigate the high risk of falls.
- Inspection and certification of scaffolding to ensure structural integrity and worker safety.
- Coverage and protection of all electrical installations to prevent electric shock and short circuits.

6. Next Steps
The construction site management is required to:
- Address the critical findings outlined above within the next 48 hours.
- Provide documentation of scaffolding inspection and certification.
- Verify the installation of fall protection at the open edge on the third floor.
- Ensure all electrical installations are properly covered and protected.
A follow-up inspection will be scheduled to verify compliance with these recommendations.
